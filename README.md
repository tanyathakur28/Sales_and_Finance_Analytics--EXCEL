**About AtliQ Hardware**

Atliq Hardware sells a variety of hardware products such as personal computers, mice, printers, and other peripheral devices. We manufacture our own products and serve a wide range of customers. Our products are sold through retail partners like Croma and Best Buy, as well as online platforms such as Flipkart and Amazon.
